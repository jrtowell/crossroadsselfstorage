Do you send out notifications when there are auctions?

Yes. You can sign up to be on the auction email list here.

How long is the minimum lease?

One month.

Do I have to sign a lease?

Yes, but the lease is month-to-month. There is no long-term commitment.

Do I bring my own lock?

Yes. We recommend the round disc locks because they are more secure.

Is there anything I can’t store?

You cannot store combustibles like gasoline, ammunition, etc. You can't store anything illegal.

When can I access my unit?

We will give you a code to the gate. That gate is operational between 7AM and 10PM, 7 days per week.

What form of payment do you accept?

Credit card or debit card are our preferred method of payment. We also accept cash, check, or money order.

Is there a security deposit?

No.

Do you have any deals, promotions, coupons, special pricing for veterans, etc.?

No. Our prices are very low, all the time and for everyone.

Do you have climate controlled or dust controlled units?

No.

Does dust get into the units?

Yes, some dust will get inside.

Do you have boat or RV storage?

No.

Where are you located?

132 Slaton Highway. If you are inside the loop, head south on Q. You'll cross under I27 and find us on the left.

Is your facility secure?

Yes. We have a secure fence, a computer-controlled gate, and security cameras. We have lighting that comes on at night.

Can I pay in advance?

Yes.

Are the contents of my storage insured?

No. Crossroads Self Storage is not liable for the contents of your unit. Contact your current auto or home insurance provider and ask about renter's insurance.
